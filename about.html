Dance Training

Brand new? Start with our 10-days intro program.

  • Expert Instruction: Our experienced instructors provide personalized guidance, ensuring dancers of all levels receive tailored feedback to enhance their skills.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: We offer a diverse range of styles and techniques, from ballet to hip hop, allowing students to explore their interests and grow as versatile dancers.
  • Performance Opportunities: Students can showcase their progress through regular recitals and competitions, building confidence and stage presence in a supportive environment.

Beginner

Got the basics down? Level up with new moves and routines.

  • Welcoming Environment: Our beginner classes are designed for newcomers, fostering a friendly atmosphere where everyone feels comfortable and encouraged to learn.
  • Fundamental Techniques: Students will focus on basic movements and foundational skills, ensuring a solid understanding of dance principles that will serve as a strong base for future growth.
  • Fun and Engaging: Classes incorporate games and creative activities, making learning enjoyable while cultivating a passion for dance from the very start.

Intermediate

Feel pretty confident? Learn more challenging skills and pieces.

  • Skill Advancement: Intermediate classes challenge dancers to refine their technique, explore more complex choreography, and develop their unique style through varied dance genres.
  • Collaborative Learning: Students engage in group work and partner exercises, enhancing their ability to work with others while fostering a sense of community among peers.
  • Performance Preparation: Dancers will have opportunities to participate in showcases and competitions, allowing them to apply their skills in a real-world setting and gain valuable performance experience.

Variety of Dance Styles

The Following are variety of dance styles we offer.

Azonto
This class emphasizes storytelling through dance, allowing you to connect with the music and culture while having fun.
Hip Hop
This high-energy class focuses on rhythm, creativity, and self-expression, combining various techniques from breaking to locking.
Lite Feet
Known for its fast-paced footwork and smooth gliding moves, this class will challenge your coordination and rhythm while encouraging you to freestyle and express yourself.
Afrobeat
This class combines traditional African dance with modern influences, creating a lively and expressive atmosphere.
